Why I Made This

I love playing the Infinite Fusion game, but I've noticed that the data in the Infinite Fusion Calculator isn't updated and the design looks outdated. So, I updated this Infinite Fusion Calculator with the latest v97 sprite pack, including 121,080 custom sprites. It now features a modern design, works on all devices responsively, and is open source.

How Infinite Fusion Calculator Works?

The Pokemon Infinite Fusion Calculator is a powerful tool designed to assist trainers in creating unique fusion combinations of Pokemon.

I have updated all the data of Pokemon up to version 6. We now have a total of 470 Pokemon, and all errors have been fixed. Now, you can calculate infinite fusions with the latest Pokemon.

When you enter the names of the Pokemon and click the calculate button, we retrieve the Pokedex IDs of both Pokemon. Using these Pokedex IDs, we access the database to retrieve images, stats, and various other calculation data.

This tool displays both Pokemon's custom sprites and autogen sprites along with their Pokedex IDs. Below the images, you will find stats, Pokemon types, and type effectiveness data against your chosen Pokemon's types.
